Services in DMZ

Some of the most common services that are placed in the DMZ are:

  1. Web Servers
  2. Mail Servers
  3. FTP Servers
  4. VoIP Servers

Any service that is being provided to users on the external network can be placed here. Anything with sensitive or private information should be kept on the internal network. The servers on the DMZ can then communicate with the internal network either directly or through an application firewall for any information that is needed. This reduces access to the internal network.
