Truck Fact Sheet


Stats

  • Average Price - $40,000+(source)
  • Ease of use / Standard features - No more than 3 kids
  • Gas mileage - 15 to 20 mpg (source)
  • Lifespan - Okay (source)

Summary and Explanation

The average price of trucks is quite high for a full-sized pick-up. They have a lot more power than is really needed to take the kids to soccer practice. They are very roomy inside, but lack a third row. So if you have 3 kids you better make sure to be diligent with the contraceptives. Otherwise plan to get a new vehicle. The gas mileage is quite bad, but that shouldn't be unexpected. The lifespan of trucks is decent, but you pay for it up front.
