Stats

  • Average Price - $30,000 (source)
  • Ease of use / Standard features - High
  • Gas mileage - 20 to 25 mpg (source)

Summary and Explanation

The average price minivans is much better than SUVs or Trucks. The ease of use can't be overstated, automatic sliding doors, DVD players, stow-and-go seating. It's a dream come true for anybody with children. The gas mileage is better than a Truck or SUV, almost like it's for family outings instead of hunting or off-road. The lifespan is very good for minivans, the Honda Odyssey in particular has received awards for this on several occasions. In short, everything you need in a family vehicle.
