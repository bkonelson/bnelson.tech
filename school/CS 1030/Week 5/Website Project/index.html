Conclusion

Mini-vans sometimes have a bad connotation. They are often associated with boring housewives, and fathers who have traded in the majority of their manliness for a life of tantrums and poopy diapers. However; after looking at the fact sheets for all three vehicle types, it is easy to see that mini-vans are much more than a signal to society that you've lost all of your sex appeal. They are quality vehicles that really fit the needs of a large group of consumers, and they fit the need well.
