Suv Fact Sheet


Stats

  • Average Price - $37,000 (source)
  • Ease of use / Standard features - Low
  • Gas mileage - 18 to 22 mpg (source)
  • Lifespan - Not great (source)

Summary and Explanation

The average price of SUVs varies quite a bit but for large SUVs it is quite high. Most of them start at around $35,000 without any options. The gas mileage is similar to a truck, very much not what is wanted for the long road trips to Disneyland. To get into the back seats is quite a challenge, and for the parent of a 2-year old it would make life quite difficult. And although there are a couple SUVs with good reliability ratings, such as the Honda CR-V, large SUVs with 3 rows of seating don't make the cut.
