Introduction

My brother-in-law was in need of a computer and I interviewed him to see what exactly he wanted. He works for a construction company which he is going to own in the future, and he also does some gaming. He needs something that he can use for the business as well as for entertainment. His wife uses the internet for pintrest, facebook, etc. and also is trying to start a small business for home decoration/crafts. Conclusion

I decided they should go with the Basic Custom PC as it will fit their needs best and their budget. Some of the top considerations were:

  • price to performance ratio
  • hard disk space and speed
  • graphics capability
  • use for business

This PC has a lot of hard disk space for their businesses and is also good for gaming. I decided to go with Windows for the operating system because although it is more expensive, it provides better drivers for video hardware and they are already familiar with how it works. They didn't have much of a need for portability, as they already have portable devices. With that in mind, the monitor being larger and graphics for the desktops ruled out the laptop. The high end PC would have met their needs plus some, but doesn't provide the level of added benefit that the price might suggest.
